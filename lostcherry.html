LOST CHERRY PROJECT (2024)

Tom Ford’s Lost Cherry – Editorial Design Project This magazine spread is a bold and immersive editorial piece designed to capture the essence of Tom Ford’s Lost Cherry fragrance. The layout balances luxury, sensuality, and intrigue, reflecting the perfume’s seductive and sophisticated nature. The visual centerpiece of the spread is a striking image of a deep red cherry pierced by a fork, symbolizing temptation, indulgence, and a hint of danger—a direct nod to the fragrance’s luscious, provocative scent profile. The large, commanding typography ("GET LOST") reinforces the idea of surrendering to the depth and allure of the perfume, drawing the reader into an evocative experience. The editorial text highlights Lost Cherry’s unique blend of rich black cherry, creamy tonka bean, and warm sandalwood, portraying it as more than just a fruity fragrance—it’s a layered, intoxicating journey that evolves over time. The article’s warm and descriptive tone complements the luxurious aesthetic, making the reader feel as though they are experiencing the scent firsthand. The overall design achieves a modern, high-fashion feel, seamlessly merging visual storytelling with fragrance journalism. The strategic use of contrast, bold typography, and elegant imagery ensures a compelling and immersive reader experience, aligning perfectly with Tom Ford’s signature style of opulence and allure. This project showcases expertise in editorial layout, luxury branding, and high-end product storytelling, making it a standout feature in any design portfolio.
